We're looking for partners

Students in the final year of a number of Federation University Australia courses take part in industry-based student projects, creating real products for real customers.

The students engage in the full project management and development life cycle with guidance from a supervisor within the School.

For students, it's an opportunity to showcase their skills, gain experience working with clients, and deliver genuine value to their community.

What's involved?

As a client for a student project, you will need to:

  • Develop a project brief to allow Federation University Australia staff to assess your project;
  • Have a clear idea of the project goals, but be willing to work with your team to set the scope and size of the project
  • Make yourself available to your student team for feedback and planning, at least once every 1-2 weeks
  • Local face-to-face contact is always valuable, however online meetings are also an option. If you have specific requirements either way, please let us know

What's it cost?

For many projects, it costs nothing!

However, if your project requires specialist equipment, software licenses or access to commercial tools that are not available to Federation University Australia students, then you may need to provide these resources for the duration of the project. For IT projects, it is often possible for students to use free, open source software to develop your system.

Federation University Australia cannot provide maintenance or support for projects after project completion. We can also provide no guarantees that the final project will meet your requirements. If the final delivered project needs additional work then you will need to engage other developers (or members of your project team) at your own cost.

What sort of projects?

Our students have successfully developed projects for schools, small and large businesses, and charities

Some examples include:

  • Administrative software for a veterinary practice
  • Educational games for schools
  • Networking systems
  • Management software for an accountancy practice
  • Fitness class scheduling systems
  • HR and Customer Management systems
  • Due to the nature of student projects, we prefer proposals that are not critical to the success of your business.
